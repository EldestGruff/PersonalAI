# Pricing Strategy & Monetization Model

**Last Updated**: 2026-01-30
**Purpose**: Define pricing tiers, revenue model, and financial sustainability

## Executive Summary

**Recommended Model**: Freemium with generous free tier + $3.99/month ($39.99/year) premium subscription

**Rationale**:
- Lower than Notion ($10/mo), Day One ($35/yr), competitive with Bear ($30/yr)
- Captures ADHD users (price-sensitive but willing to pay for value)
- Annual pricing encourages commitment (20% discount)
- Free tier builds user base for word-of-mouth growth

**Target Financials (Year 1)**:
- 20,000 free users
- 2,000 paid users (10% conversion)
- $96,000 ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)
- Break-even: ~800 paid users

## Pricing Tiers

### Free Tier: "PersonalAI Starter"
**Price**: $0

**Includes**:
- ✅ **50 thoughts per month** (resets monthly)
- ✅ **AI classification** (all 5 types: note, idea, task, event, question)
- ✅ **Voice capture** (Siri integration)
- ✅ **Basic search** (text search, no semantic search)
- ✅ **Context enrichment** (location, energy, focus state)
- ✅ **Manual tags** (add/remove tags)
- ✅ **Calendar/Reminder creation** (one-tap)
- ✅ **iCloud backup** (automatic)
- ❌ Auto-tagging (AI-suggested tags)
- ❌ Charts and insights
- ❌ Export (JSON, Markdown, CSV)
- ❌ Unlimited thoughts
- ❌ Advanced search (semantic, filters)
- ❌ Custom classification training

**Why This Works**:
- 50 thoughts/month = ~1.7 per day (reasonable for testing)
- Core value prop visible (AI classification, context)
- Enough to validate usefulness, not enough for power users
- Natural upgrade trigger when limit hit

**Target User**: Curious triers, light users, students

---

### Premium Tier: "PersonalAI Pro"
**Price**: $3.99/month or $39.99/year (17% savings)

**Includes Everything in Free, Plus**:
- ✅ **Unlimited thoughts**
- ✅ **AI auto-tagging** (suggested tags based on content)
- ✅ **Charts and insights** (sentiment trends, type distribution, productivity patterns)
- ✅ **Advanced search** (semantic search, multi-filter, date ranges)
- ✅ **Health correlation insights** (energy × productivity patterns)
- ✅ **Export capabilities** (JSON, Markdown, CSV formats)
- ✅ **Priority support** (email response within 24 hours)
- ✅ **Early access** (beta features before free users)
- ✅ **Custom classification feedback** (improve AI with corrections)

**Why This Works**:
- $3.99/mo = cost of a coffee, easy impulse purchase
- $39.99/yr = $3.33/mo effective, appeals to annual buyers
- Unlocks all differentiators (charts, insights, health correlation)
- Justifies "Pro" label without being expensive

**Target User**: ADHD users, knowledge workers, health trackers (primary audiences)

---

### Future Tier: "PersonalAI Plus" (Phase 2, 6-12 months)
**Price**: $7.99/month or $79.99/year

**Includes Everything in Pro, Plus**:
- ✅ **Multi-device sync** (via CloudKit or custom backend)
- ✅ **Web app access** (view/edit from browser)
- ✅ **Shared thoughts** (collaborate with family/team)
- ✅ **API access** (integrate with Zapier, Make, custom workflows)
- ✅ **Advanced AI models** (fine-tuned on your data for better classification)
- ✅ **Custom integrations** (Slack, Discord, email)
- ✅ **Priority AI processing** (faster classification)
- ✅ **Longer history** (unlimited retention vs. 1 year for free)

**Why This Exists**:
- Upsell path for power users
- Team/collaboration features command premium
- API access = developer audience
- Revenue diversification

**Target User**: Teams, power users, developers (secondary market)

## Free Trial Strategy

### Trial Duration: 7 Days
**Why 7 days**:
- Long enough to form habit (capture 5-10 thoughts)
- Short enough to create urgency
- Industry standard for mobile subscriptions

### Trial Experience
1. **Day 0 (Install)**: Onboarding emphasizes premium features
2. **Day 1**: Push notification: "Capture your first thought with Siri"
3. **Day 3**: Email: "You've captured X thoughts—see your insights"
4. **Day 5**: Push: "2 days left in trial—view your productivity patterns"
5. **Day 6**: Email: "Last day to keep unlimited thoughts"
6. **Day 7**: In-app prompt at thought #51: "Upgrade to continue capturing"

**Conversion Goal**: 15-20% trial-to-paid (industry standard for productivity apps)

---

## Upgrade Triggers (Free → Pro)

### Hard Limits (Blockers)
1. **50 thoughts reached**: "You've reached your monthly limit. Upgrade for unlimited thoughts."
2. **Insights locked**: Tap on Charts → "Unlock insights with PersonalAI Pro"
3. **Export locked**: "Export requires PersonalAI Pro"

### Soft Prompts (Encouragement)
1. **After 10 thoughts**: "You're off to a great start! See your patterns with Pro insights."
2. **After classification correction**: "Help improve AI with Pro feedback features"
3. **After granting Health permission**: "Unlock energy correlation insights with Pro"

### Contextual Upsells
- **Low energy day**: "Pro users see when they're most productive. Upgrade?"
- **Frequent searching**: "Pro advanced search finds thoughts 3x faster"
- **Multiple tags**: "Pro suggests tags automatically. Save time?"

## Discount & Promotion Strategy

### Launch Promotion (First 6 months)
**Lifetime Deal**: $99 one-time (limited to first 500 users)
- Creates urgency and scarcity
- Generates $49,500 upfront revenue
- Builds core advocate base
- Limits downside (only 500 users)

**Why**: Lifetime deals work for early adopters who take a chance on unproven apps

---

### Seasonal Discounts
1. **Back to School** (August-September): 20% off annual ($31.99)
2. **Black Friday** (November): 30% off annual ($27.99)
3. **New Year** (January): "New year, new mind" 25% off annual ($29.99)

**Why**: Aligns with productivity/self-improvement moments

---

### Student Discount: 50% off annual ($19.99/year)
**Verification**: via UNiDAYS or SheerID
**Why**: Students are price-sensitive but become lifelong users
**CAC**: Near-zero (word of mouth on campus)

---

### Referral Program (Phase 2)
**Give**: 1 month free Pro
**Get**: 1 month free Pro (when referred user subscribes)

**Why**: Incentivizes word-of-mouth, low CAC

## Break-Even Analysis

### Fixed Monthly Costs (Estimated)
- **OpenAI API**: $500-2,000 (usage-based, see COST_ANALYSIS.md)
- **Infrastructure**: $50 (TestFlight, analytics, domain)
- **App Store Fee**: 30% of revenue (variable)
- **Support**: $0 (founder-led initially)
- **Marketing**: $500-1,000 (content, ads)

**Total Fixed**: ~$1,050-3,050/month

### Break-Even Subscribers
At $3.99/month ($2.79 after App Store cut):
- **Conservative**: ~800 paid users ($2,232 MRR)
- **With marketing**: ~1,200 paid users ($3,348 MRR)

At $39.99/year ($27.99 after App Store cut, $2.33/mo effective):
- **Conservative**: ~950 paid users
- **With marketing**: ~1,400 paid users

**Realistic Timeline to Break-Even**: Month 6-9

## Pricing Experimentation Plan

### Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Validate Baseline
- Launch at $3.99/mo, $39.99/yr
- Measure conversion at 50-thought limit
- Track which features drive upgrades
- Collect willingness-to-pay surveys

### Phase 2 (Months 4-6): Test Price Elasticity
- **A/B test**: $2.99/mo vs. $4.99/mo (small cohorts)
- **A/B test**: Annual discount 15% vs. 25%
- **A/B test**: Free tier limit 30 vs. 50 vs. 100 thoughts

### Phase 3 (Months 7-12): Optimize
- Adjust based on data (likely: raise price if demand strong)
- Introduce Plus tier if power user demand exists
- Test regional pricing (US vs. EU vs. emerging markets)

## Refund & Cancellation Policy

### App Store Standard
- **7-day trial**: Cancel anytime, no charge
- **Cancellation**: Remains active until period ends, no pro-rata refunds
- **Refunds**: Apple handles (14-day window, case-by-case)

### Our Policy (Customer-Friendly)
- **Email requests**: Honor refunds within 30 days, no questions
- **Churn prevention**: Offer 3 months at 50% off before canceling
- **Win-back**: Email after 60 days with 1-month free trial offer

**Why**: Goodwill > short-term revenue, reduces negative reviews

## Metrics to Track

### Conversion Funnel
1. **Free sign-ups** → Track sources (Product Hunt, Reddit, etc.)
2. **Trial starts** → % of free users who start trial
3. **Trial engagement** → Thoughts captured during trial
4. **Trial conversions** → % who subscribe after 7 days
5. **Churn rate** → % who cancel monthly

### Revenue Metrics
- **MRR** (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- **ARR** (Annual Recurring Revenue)
- **ARPU** (Average Revenue Per User)
- **LTV** (Lifetime Value = ARPU / churn rate)
- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **LTV:CAC ratio** (target 3:1 minimum)

### Feature Adoption
- Which premium features are used most?
- Which upgrade triggers convert best?
- Do insight viewers convert at higher rates?
