## Quick Capture

### Voice via Siri
Say: **"Hey Siri, stash a thought in STASH"**

Siri asks "What's on your mind?" — just speak your thought.

**Pro tip:** Create a custom Siri phrase:
1. Open Shortcuts app
2. Find "Stash Thought"
3. Add to Siri with a shorter phrase like "stash it"

### Back Tap (Double-tap your phone)
**Setup:** Settings → Accessibility → Touch → Back Tap → Double Tap → Shortcuts → Voice Capture

Now double-tap the back of your phone to instantly start voice capture.

## Permissions Unlock Features

Grant these for richer context:

- **Location** → Thoughts tagged with where you were
- **Calendar** → Connect thoughts to meetings/events
- **Health** → Correlate mood with activity

**Enable:** Settings → Privacy

---

## Search Like a Pro

- **Semantic search** finds related thoughts, not just keywords
- **Filter by tags** — tap any tag to see all tagged thoughts
- **Date ranges** — swipe to filter by week/month
- Say **"Hey Siri, search my thoughts for [topic] in STASH"**

## Hidden Features

### Streak Tracking
**Insights tab** shows your daily capture streak. Build the habit!

### Auto-Classification
STASH auto-detects:
- Note vs. reminder vs. task
- Sentiment (positive/negative/neutral)
- Suggested tags

### Context Enrichment
Every thought captures:
- Time of day
- Location (if enabled)
- Calendar events
- Activity (walking/sitting)
- Weather

## Troubleshooting

**"Something went wrong" with Siri:**
- Launch STASH once
- Wait 2-3 minutes
- Try again

**Back Tap not working:**
- Remove thick phone case
- Tap firmly, but not hard
- Check Settings → Accessibility → Touch → Back Tap

**Voice permissions:**
Settings → Privacy → Microphone/Speech Recognition → enable STASH
