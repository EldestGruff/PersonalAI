# STASH Quick Setup Guide

Get started with STASH in under 5 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Grant Permissions (30 seconds)

On first launch, STASH will request:

- ✅ **Siri & Shortcuts** — Required for voice capture
- ✅ **Microphone** — Required for voice input
- ✅ **Speech Recognition** — Required for transcription
- ⚪ **Location** (Optional) — Enriches thoughts with location context
- ⚪ **Calendar** (Optional) — Connects thoughts to your schedule
- ⚪ **HealthKit** (Optional) — Correlates thoughts with activity/mood

**Tap "Allow" for all.** You can adjust later in Settings.

---

## Step 2: Enable Siri (1 minute)

### Test the basic command:

Say: **"Hey Siri, stash a thought in STASH"**

Siri should respond: **"What's on your mind?"**

Speak a test thought. Siri saves it automatically.

### If it doesn't work:

1. Open **Settings** → **Siri & Search** → **STASH**
2. Enable **"Use with Siri"**
3. Wait 2 minutes, try again

---

## Step 3: Set Up Back Tap (2 minutes)

**The fastest way to capture thoughts:**

1. Open **Settings** → **Accessibility** → **Touch** → **Back Tap**
2. Choose **Double Tap**
3. Scroll to **Shortcuts** section
4. Select **Voice Capture** (under STASH)

**Test it:** Double-tap the back of your phone. STASH should open with voice capture ready.

---

## Step 4: Create a Custom Siri Phrase (Optional, 1 minute)

**Make it shorter:**

1. Open **Shortcuts** app
2. Find **"Stash Thought"** under STASH
3. Tap (•••) → **Add to Siri**
4. Record a custom phrase: "stash it" or "quick stash"

Now you can say your custom phrase instead of the full "stash a thought in STASH."

---

## Step 5: Capture Your First Real Thought

**Via Siri:**
- "Hey Siri, stash a thought in STASH"
- Speak your thought
- Done ✓

**Via Back Tap:**
- Double-tap back of phone
- Speak your thought
- Tap "Done" or wait 3 seconds for auto-save
- Done ✓

---

## You're All Set! 🎉

**What's next:**

- Browse your thoughts in the **Browse** tab
- View AI insights in the **Insights** tab
- Customize themes in **Settings**
- Read [Power User Tips](POWER_USER_TIPS.md) for advanced workflows

## Common First-Time Issues

### "Something went wrong" with Siri

- Launch the app at least once
- Wait 2-3 minutes for iOS to index shortcuts
- Restart your phone if needed

### Back Tap not responding

- Remove thick phone case
- Tap firmly (but not too hard)
- Check Settings → Accessibility → Touch → Back Tap

### Voice capture permission error

- Settings → Privacy → Microphone → enable STASH
- Settings → Privacy → Speech Recognition → enable STASH
